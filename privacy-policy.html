Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 13, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal data to provide and improve our services:

1.1 Account Information

  • Email address
  • Authentication data (encrypted)
  • Profile preferences

1.2 Usage Data

  • Saved analyses and predictions
  • App settings and preferences
  • Device information (for authentication)

1.3 Analytics Data (with consent)

  • App usage statistics
  • Performance metrics
  • Crash reports

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Providing and maintaining our services
  • Authenticating your access to the app
  • Saving and retrieving your analyses
  • Sending notifications (with your consent)
  • Improving our services (with your consent)
  • Analyzing app performance (with your consent)

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

3.1 Contractual Necessity

  • Account authentication and management
  • Saving and accessing your analyses

3.2 Consent

  • Analytics and usage tracking
  • Marketing communications
  • Personalization features

3.3 Legitimate Interest

  • Security and fraud prevention
  • App optimization and performance
  • Error logging and debugging

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share your data only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party services that power our app (Supabase for database, RevenueCat for subscriptions, Sentry for error tracking)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law to comply with legal obligations
  • Business Transfer: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal data:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest (Supabase encryption)
  • Authentication: Secure login with biometric support (Face ID/Touch ID)
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and authentication for our systems
  • Regular Audits: Regular security reviews and updates

6. Your GDPR Rights

You have the following rights under GDPR:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interest

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@parlaywizard.app or use the in-app data export/delete features.

7. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained until account deletion
  • Saved Analyses: Retained until account deletion
  • Analytics Data: Retained for 2 years after last interaction
  • Consent Records: Retained for 2 years after last update

8. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication (EU region)
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management
  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • Sentry: Error tracking and monitoring
  • Mixpanel: Analytics (opt-in only)

Each service has its own privacy policy and data handling practices.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data is stored in the EU (Supabase EU region). We do not transfer your data outside the EU without adequate safeguards.

10. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 12. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 12. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete it immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Sending you an in-app notification
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
